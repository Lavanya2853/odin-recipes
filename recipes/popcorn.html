Pop Corn

Description

A Sweet,salty pop corn is best snack ever!

Ingredients

  • Kernals
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Start with poping the kernels into pop corns
  2. In a sauce pan add sugar and caramelise it well
  3. Also add a pinch of salt to it
  4. Add a cube of butter to the caramel
  5. Finallyadd the pop corns and mix them well
