Lasagna

Description

A Delicious,cheesy Lasagna is a bless is the bliss!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Start with the sauce preparation and get the required Ingredients.
  2. Get microwavable container and grate the cheese aside.
  3. Put a layer of sauce in the container and place the pasta sheets.
  4. Repeat the step 3 until the container in 3/4th filled and finish it with the grated cheese.
  5. Microwave for 30 mins until the cheese is completely cooked and melt.
