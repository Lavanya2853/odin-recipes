Noodles

Description

A beautifull noodles that are a treat to our eyes is never a compliment

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Spices
  • Chickhen stock
  • Meat
  • Egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Start with boiling the noodles and cool it aside
  2. Stir fry the spices and meat
  3. Pour the chickhen stock and bring it to boil
  4. Add the boiled noodles
  5. Finally garnish it with fried egg and Green onions
