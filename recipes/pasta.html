Pasta

Description

A Healthy,fresh pasta is a yummy option even to think. Enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Veggies
  • Dressing
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Start with boiling the pasta and strain. Add a teaspoon of oil to mix and cool the pasta.
  2. Stir fry the Veggies until its cooked.
  3. Add the sauce to the pan with Veggies and cook for few minutes.
  4. Add the boiled pasta and mix all the Ingredients well.
  5. Garnish with some Dressing and green onions.
