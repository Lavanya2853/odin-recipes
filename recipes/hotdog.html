Hot Dog

Description

A cheesy Hot Dog is the best goto snack for a firld trip!

Ingredients

  • Hot dog Buns
  • Sausages
  • Hot sauce ,Mustard Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start with grilling the Sausages in a grill pan.
  2. Then toast the buns with butter on it.
  3. Place the grilled Sausages in the buns with some lettues
  4. Add the hot sauce and Mustard
  5. Finally add some grated cheese and
